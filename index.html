Featured Combos

The Spicy Showdown

The Spicy Showdown features a fiery Chicken Tikka Masala Pizza with red onions, jalapeños, and cilantro drizzle. To balance the heat, the menu includes a creamy and cooling Mango Lassi drink. The side dish is a crispy and savory Tandoori Chicken Calzone filled with flavorful tandoori chicken and onions.

Veggie Fiesta

The Veggie Fiesta menu features a Veggie Supreme Pizza with peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, and spinach. The drink is a Freshly Squeezed Mint Lemonade that is refreshing and revitalizing. The side dish is a light and creamy Pesto Spinach & Ricotta Pasta tossed with vibrant pesto, spinach, and ricotta.

Classic Comfort

The Classic Comfort menu features a Classic Margherita Pizza with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil. The drink is a classic Coca-Cola, which is a perfect pairing for the pizza. The side dish is warm, buttery Garlic Breadsticks that are perfect for dipping.
