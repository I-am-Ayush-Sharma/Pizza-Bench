About Us

Introduction

Craving a slice of the good life? Pull up a seat at Pizza Bench, your one-stop shop for handcrafted pizzas that hit the spot every time.

Imagine a sun-drenched picnic table, laden with steaming pizzas, laughter bubbling like the cheese in the crust. That's the feeling we're going for at Pizza Bench. We're not just slinging pies; we're creating experiences.

Our dough is made with love (and a little secret ingredient), hand-tossed to airy perfection. We pile on the freshest toppings, from classic mozzarella and pepperoni to adventurous combos like fig and prosciutto or BBQ chicken with pineapple (don't knock it till you try it!).

But Pizza Bench is more than just the food. It's a vibe. It's a place to gather with friends, family, or even just yourself (we won't judge your solo pizza party). It's a place to unwind, recharge, and savor the simple pleasures in life.

So, come on over and grab a seat at our virtual bench. We've got a slice (or ten) waiting for you.

Order now and let the good times roll!

Meet Our Chefs

Marco Rossi

Marco, or "Marco Polo of the Pizzaverse" as his colleagues like to call him, brings a wealth of international experience to the Pizza Bench kitchen. After honing his craft in pizzerias across Italy, France, and New York City, Marco has landed in Delhi, eager to share his passion for fresh, seasonal ingredients and innovative flavor combinations. His signature dish? The "Globe Trotter," a symphony of mozzarella, prosciutto, roasted peppers, and a touch of truffle oil that will transport your taste buds around the world.

Christa Fritz

Don't let Christa's youthful appearance fool you – she's a master of the dough! Her understanding of gluten development and fermentation is nothing short of magic, resulting in airy, pillowy crusts that are the perfect canvas for any topping. Christa's not afraid to experiment, and her "Deconstructed Margherita" – a playful take on the classic with dollops of fresh tomato sauce, torn basil leaves, and fior di latte scattered around the crust – is a testament to her culinary creativity.

Carlos Diaz

Spice up your life with Carlos's fiery creations! Hailing from Mexico, Carlos infuses his pizzas with the warmth and vibrancy of his heritage. His "Dia de los Muertos" pizza is a showstopper, with a base of chipotle crema, topped with pulled chicken, caramelized onions, and a generous sprinkle of cilantro. Don't be intimidated by the heat – Carlos's spice is always balanced, leaving you wanting more with every bite.
